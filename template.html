YOUR FARMER Cliff Rodriguez

I am first generation farmer committed to learning and creating abundance. Sweet peppers, amaranth, and radishes are among my most beloved crops. My farming passion has been cultivating itself since I was 5 and was the preferred gardener for all the neighbors on the block. Each day I am eager to participate in the web of life.

CURRENT FOCUSES:

  • evergreen method
  • intensive crop harvesting
  • indoor kelp cultivation
  • medium scale integrated permaculture installations
  • cultiavtion of endangered plant species
  • orchid and succulent cultivation

I am in the process of working on a plot of land located in the San Louis Valley of Colorado. Follow Farm in the Valley for updates on my new plot of land.
